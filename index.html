About CartLu

Connecting communities, one fresh delivery at a time.

CartLu was born from a simple idea: to bridge the gap between local fruit and vegetable hawkers and the modern consumer. We saw an opportunity to leverage technology not to replace, but to empower these hardworking individuals who are the backbone of our local food supply.

Our platform provides hawkers with the digital tools they need to expand their reach, manage their inventory efficiently, and connect with a wider customer base. For you, the consumer, CartLu means access to the freshest produce, the convenience of online ordering, and the satisfaction of supporting your local community.

We believe in fair trade, sustainable practices, and the power of technology to create a positive impact. Join us in nurturing a more vibrant and equitable local food ecosystem.

How CartLu Works

Simple, seamless, and supportive.

1. Hawkers Onboard

Local hawkers register and list their fresh inventory and location on the CartLu platform with ease.

2. Customers Discover

You browse nearby hawkers, view their real-time produce selection, and place orders through our user-friendly app.

3. Freshness Delivered/Picked

Choose convenient delivery to your doorstep or quick pickup from your favorite hawker.

4. Community Thrives

Hawkers gain more sales and operational efficiency, while you enjoy fresh food and support local businesses.
